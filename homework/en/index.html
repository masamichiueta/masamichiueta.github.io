Homework

A study timer kids run themselves

Children record the time they spend on homework, juku (cram school), or Kumon — all by themselves. A gentle, hiragana-first interface means even a five-year-old can use it. Streaks, levels, charts, and 300 badges make every day's effort visible.

What you get

Record it → see it → keep it going

⏱️

Timer & manual entry

Tap once to start timing. Forgot to time a session? Add it by hand later.

🔥

Streaks

A day with at least a minute counts. Today, current streak, and this week are always one glance away.

⭐

Levels

Climb a level as your study time adds up. The home screen counts down to the next level-up.

📊

Charts & heatmap

Stacked bar charts by week or month, pageable into the past, plus a 13-week heatmap for the long view.

🏅

300 badges

Across 27 categories. The home screen shows the next badges you're closest to earning.

📜

Certificate

Turn a month or a year of effort into an A4 PDF — for the fridge or the teacher.

🔔

Reminders (this device only)

Per-child reminders, stored only on the device. Auto-skipped once they've already studied that day.

🔒

Parent mode & PIN

Protect editing and management. The optional 4-digit PIN stays on this device only.

☁️

iCloud sync

Automatic across devices on the same Apple ID. Share across family Apple IDs with a file.

🛡️

Privacy

No ad SDKs, no analytics. Your data lives only on your device and your own iCloud.
