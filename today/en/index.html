Today Score Everyday

Rate your day from 1 to 10

How was today? Pick a number from 1 to 10, add a quick note, and you're done in seconds. A simple reflection app that lets you look back with monthly history, a calendar, and stats like your average, streak, and a trend chart.

Download on the App Store
  • iOS 26.5+
  • iPhone & iPad
  • Japanese & English
  • No ads · No tracking

What you get

Record → look back → see your rhythm

🔢

Record in a few taps

Choose today's score from 1 to 10. The color and emoji change with your score (from "rough" to "great"), so your day is clear at a glance. Add a short note too.

🗓️

Look back through history

A colorful list grouped by month. Switch between list and calendar (heatmap), tap to edit, swipe to delete. Revisit any day, anytime.

📊

See your rhythm in stats

Average score, current streak, and total days, plus a trend chart and averages by weekday and month — the ups and downs become visible.

🔔

Daily reminder

A gentle notification at the time you choose. You can log today's score straight from the notification, so you never forget to check in.

☁️

iCloud sync

Automatically syncs across devices on the same Apple Account. Your records follow you to a new phone.

How it works

Three steps, just a few seconds a day

1

Pick a score

Rate how today felt, from 1 to 10.

2

Add a note

One line, optional. Then tap Save.

3

Look back

History, a calendar, and stats make your rhythm visible.
