AspectFit

Extend photos to preset ratios

Reshape any photo to the exact aspect ratio you need — no awkward cropping, no ugly bars. AspectFit fills the new space with a background generated from your own image, so your photo fits wallpapers and social posts.

Coming soon to the App Store

  • iPhone & iPad
  • Japanese & English
  • No ads · No tracking

What you get

Change the ratio — no cropping, no ugly bars

Ready-made ratios

Presets for wallpapers (iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch) and social (Story/Reels, Square, Portrait, Widescreen).

🎨

Six background styles

Gradient, Multi Gradient, Blur, Stretch, Soft Stretch, and Solid color — generated from your own image to blend in naturally.

Drag to position

Fine-tune your photo's position with a drag. One tap re-centers it whenever you want.

Light & Dark

Designed for Light and Dark Mode and Dynamic Type — clear and comfortable for everyone.

Fully on-device

All editing happens on your device. No account, no ads, nothing sent off your phone.

How it works

Three steps to the perfect aspect ratio

1

Pick a photo

Choose the photo you want to reshape from the iOS photo picker.

2

Choose ratio & background

Select a target aspect ratio and background style, then drag to position.

3

Save & share

Save the generated image to Photos, or share it straight from the share sheet.
